Example :

Input co-ordinates are A(2,3) and B(12,8)


Using two-point form to calculate slope i.e
Slope(m) = (y2-y1) / (x2 - x1) 

	Therefore Slope of line AB (mAB) = (8 - 3) / (12 - 2)
			         	  = 5 / 10
					= 0.5
As mAB < 1 Increment x by 1
Increment y by 0.5
